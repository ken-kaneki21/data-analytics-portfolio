# EV / Hybrid Vehicle Market Study – India

## Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end market analysis of the Electric and Hybrid Vehicle (EV/Hybrid) ecosystem in India. The objective is to understand market trends, regional adoption patterns, manufacturer performance, and infrastructure readiness using publicly available data.

The analysis focuses on identifying growth opportunities and competitive dynamics that can support strategic decision-making for market entry and expansion in the Indian EV sector.

---

## Project Objectives
- Analyze EV market trends and growth patterns across India
- Evaluate state-wise EV adoption and penetration levels
- Examine market share and performance of leading EV manufacturers
- Assess public EV charging infrastructure distribution across states
- Provide data-driven insights and strategic recommendations

---

## Key Findings
- **Market Concentration:** EV sales are concentrated among a limited set of manufacturers, especially in the four-wheeler segment.
- **Regional Adoption:** EV penetration varies significantly by state, with a few regions demonstrating much higher adoption than the national average.
- **Infrastructure Readiness:** Higher adoption states generally align with better public charging infrastructure availability.
- **Vehicle Preference:** Two-wheelers account for the majority of EV sales, reflecting demand for affordable urban mobility.

---

## Strategic Insights & Recommendations
- Localize product and pricing strategy for Indian market conditions
- Leverage government incentives and subsidy programs to improve adoption
- Prioritize high-adoption states for phased market entry
- Align charging infrastructure expansion with regional demand patterns

---

## Key Analysis Questions
- What is the current EV/Hybrid market landscape in India?
- Which states show the highest EV adoption and growth potential?
- Who are the leading manufacturers and how is market share distributed?
- How does charging infrastructure availability vary across regions?

## Analysis Notes
- The dataset is monthly and includes state-wise EV sales, maker-wise EV sales, a date dimension, and public charging station counts.
- Common derived metrics used in analysis include:
  - **Penetration Rate** = (EV Sold / Total Vehicles Sold) × 100
  - **CAGR** for multi-year growth comparisons
