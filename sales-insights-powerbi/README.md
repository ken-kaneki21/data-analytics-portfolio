## Objective
To analyze sales performance across markets, customers, and time to support data-driven business decisions.

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Sales Quantity
- Profit Margin
- Revenue Contribution %

## Dashboard Features
- Market-wise and customer-wise revenue contribution
- Monthly revenue trend analysis
- Profit margin comparison across markets
- Interactive date-based filtering

## Key Insights
- Revenue is highly concentrated in a few key markets such as Delhi and Mumbai.
- Monthly revenue shows a decline in later months, indicating potential demand slowdown.
- A small group of customers contributes a large share of total revenue.
